From the Author

Chen Bobby Kim Ling

Digital Artist | International Award Winner

As a digital artist who's painted with light across international galleries, I've won awards for merging tradition with technology. But this project? It's the work I was born to create.

I'm ethnic Chinese, raised between worlds. My grandmother whispered tales of Confucius like bedtime stories; my teachers preached Silicon Valley innovation. I craved a story where ancient wisdom didn't gather dust — where ren (benevolence) and yi (justice) could duel with the urgency of a superhero saga.
